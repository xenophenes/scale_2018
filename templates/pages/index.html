PgDay SCALE 2018

- Don't Mess with Postgres -


August 26, 2017

PostgreSQL(at)SCaLE is a two day, two track event which takes place on March 8-9, 2018, at Pasadena Convention Center, as part of SCaLE 16X.

SCaLE is a completely volunteer-driven event, and in the last decade it has grown into the largest volunteer-run Linux/FOSS event in North America. SCaLE 16X takes place on March 8-11, 2018.

We’re looking for presentations surrounding experiences, tools and technical details of how you use PostgreSQL.

Anyone and everyone in the community is encouraged to submit! Presenters and talks should be technical in nature, not sales/marketing. If you’re working with PostgreSQL, we want to hear from you.

Simply go to the SCaLE website, create or login to your account, and walk through the steps of the CFP interface here. Be sure to select "PostgreSQL" as your submission's topic.

We look forward to seeing you in March!
